# Computer Observing Itself (2015)

In early 2015, I started playing with a custom camera feedback tool built on Processing. I plugged a webcam to my laptop and projected the camera feed in a 4 way mirror using a projector. As I pointed back the camera to the projection of its own perception, an ever evolving fractalic dimension started to unfold. The slight of hand became like a tool to travel into a multi-dimensional journey.

"Computer Observing Itself" is a gallery of some of the landscapes I captured during these journeys. In later stages of development I introduced a second webcam which I blended with the first.

This collection was first published in early 2016 on [Tumblr](https://ucodia.tumblr.com/tagged/feedback).
